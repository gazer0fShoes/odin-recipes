Yakisoba

Description

It's time for my personal favorite Japanese festival food! Yakisoba (Japanese stir-fried noodles) are a mainstay at any matsuri in japan, large or local! This quick, easy and delicious meal makes for a perfect party food, or even a lazy weekday dinner. And don't fret - we won't be making as much as in the picture above!

serves 3

Ingredients

  • 500g yakisoba noodles
  • ⅓ cup yakisoba sauce
  • 340g pork belly
  • ½ onion
  • ½ carrot
  • ¼ green cabbage
  • 2 green onions
  • 3 shiitake mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • black
  • aonori (optional)
  • pickled red ginger (optional)
